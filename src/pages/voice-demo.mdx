## What You'll Experience

- **Natural voice input** through your microphone
- **Real-time AI responses** with < 1.5 second latency
- **Conversational memory** maintaining context
- **Indiana-focused knowledge** base

## Technical Notes

This demo represents the core conversational pipeline:
- Speech-to-text processing (Whisper)
- LLM inference (GPT-4)
- Text-to-speech synthesis (ElevenLabs)

The full installation will add:
- Holographic display (LED rotor arrays or Pepper's Ghost)
- Particle-based avatar visualization
- Environmental lighting synchronization
- Persona-specific voice models
